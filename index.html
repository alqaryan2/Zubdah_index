How Zubdah works

Get a clean recipe from any cooking video in three simple steps.

1

Paste the video link

Copy the link of any cooking video from TikTok, Reels, or YouTube into Zubdah.

2

Let Zubdah do the work

Zubdah extracts ingredients and steps and builds a clean, readable recipe for you.

3

Cook with a clean recipe

Use your phone or tablet in the kitchen with a step-by-step view that’s easy to follow.
